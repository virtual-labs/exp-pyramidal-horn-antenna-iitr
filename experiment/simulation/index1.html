To plot the Radiation Pattern of a Pyramidal Horn Antenna.          

Click on Components button for components to pop up.
Click on the component, drag them to the test bench and place them as shown in the experiment setup.
After placing the components on the test bench, connect the wires accordingly.
To check your connections click on "Check Connections" button.
If your connections are correct then you may continue to take down the readings, if not then click on "Reset Button" and try it again.
Once you're done with the connections, you'll notice voltage for Horn Antenna at 0 degree on millivoltmeter.
Note down that value for Receiving voltage and click on "Calculate" button to calculate gain at that particular degree.
Click on Add to table to take down the values of Gain on that particular degree.
Now click on "+" button and Repeat the process for 10 to 90 degree (for anticlockwise direction) as you did for the first reading.
Or click on "-" button and Repeat the process for 360 to 270 degree (for clockwise direction) as you did for the first reading.
Note down all the readings for anticlockwise direction (0 to 90 degree) as well as for clockwise direction (360 to 270 degree).
Click on "Print" button to get your page (readings and connections) in a PDF form.
Click on "Plot" button to plot a graph as per your readings.
If you want to save the graph click on the camera icon just above the graph.
